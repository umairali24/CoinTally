# Privacy Policy for CoinTally

**Last Updated: March 31, 2026**

At CoinTally, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and the measures we take to ensure your data remains secure and private.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Local Data Storage
All of your financial records, budgets, and Zakat calculations are stored locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. We do not collect, view, or store this sensitive financial data on our servers. Your financial information remains entirely under your control.

### 1.2 Authentication
CoinTally uses Google Sign-In for secure account creation and management. When you sign in with Google, we only collect basic profile information, specifically your **Name** and **Email address**. This information is used solely to identify your account and personalize your experience.

### 1.3 SMS and Notification Parsing (Auto-Detect Transactions)
To provide the "Auto-Detect Transactions" feature, CoinTally requests **Notification Access** to read incoming bank SMS alerts and financial notifications. 

**CRITICAL PRIVACY GUARANTEE:**
*   **Local Processing:** All parsing of SMS and notification data happens **100% locally** on your device.
*   **No Data Upload:** Your SMS messages and notification contents are **NEVER uploaded**, stored on our servers, or shared with any third parties.
*   **Purpose-Specific:** The app only extracts transaction details (amount, merchant, date) from pre-defined bank-related notifications to help you manage your finances more efficiently.

### 1.4 Optional Google Drive Backups
CoinTally provides an optional Google Drive backup and restore feature so you can keep a copy of your local app data in your own Google Drive account.

*   **User-Controlled:** Backups are created only when you choose to use the backup feature.
*   **Direct to Your Google Drive:** Backup files are transferred directly between your device and your personal Google Drive account.
*   **No Server Access:** CoinTally does **not** host, store, inspect, or transmit your backup files through our servers. We do not have access to the contents of your Google Drive backups.

### 1.5 Analytics and Crash Reporting
We use **Google Firebase Analytics** to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This help us identify bugs and understand how users interact with the app so we can continue to improve the service. This data is aggregated and does not contain any personally identifiable financial information.

## 2. Data Security
We prioritize the security of your data. By keeping the vast majority of your information on your device, we minimize the risk of data breaches. We encourage users to use the app's built-in Biometric Lock feature for an additional layer of local security.

## 3. Third-Party Services
CoinTally integrates with the following third-party services:
*   **Google Services:** For authentication (Google Sign-In), analytics (Firebase), and optional user-controlled Google Drive backups. These services have their own privacy policies.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this screen and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
